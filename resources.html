Earthquake Preparedness & Response

Earthquakes can strike without warning, making preparedness essential. Our AI system analyzes seismic data to provide personalized recommendations for your location.

Before an Earthquake

  • Secure heavy furniture to walls
  • Practice "Drop, Cover, and Hold On" drills
  • Identify safe spots in each room
  • Prepare an emergency kit with extra supplies

During an Earthquake

  • Drop to the ground immediately
  • Take cover under sturdy furniture
  • Hold on until shaking stops
  • Stay away from windows and exterior walls
