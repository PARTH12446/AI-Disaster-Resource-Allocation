Data Sources & Methodology

Our prediction system integrates data from multiple sources to ensure comprehensive coverage and accuracy:

  • Satellite Imagery: High-resolution data from NASA, ESA, and commercial providers
  • Weather Stations: Real-time meteorological data from global networks
  • Seismic Sensors: Ground movement data from geological survey organizations
  • Ocean Buoys: Sea temperature, wave height, and current data
  • Social Media: Real-time public reports and images (processed with NLP)
  • Historical Data: Decades of historical disaster records and patterns
  • Government Databases: Official records from disaster management agencies worldwide
  • IoT Sensors: Network of ground sensors in high-risk areas

Our machine learning models process this data using convolutional neural networks for image analysis, recurrent neural networks for time-series prediction, and transformer models for natural language processing of textual reports.
